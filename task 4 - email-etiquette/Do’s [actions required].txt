Do’s [actions required]


 Review and update your privacy settings on all social media accounts
 Check the accuracy of a post before sharing it (use reliable sources)
 Post at least one positive or educational content per week
 Follow academic, career, or skill-based pages/groups
 Think for 10 seconds before posting or commenting


Don’ts [avoid actions]  

 Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or passwords
 Do not post or forward unverified news or rumors
 Do not engage in online fights, trolling, or negative comments
 Do not upload inappropriate photos or content
 Do not spend more than a fixed time limit (e.g., 1–2 hours/day) on social media
