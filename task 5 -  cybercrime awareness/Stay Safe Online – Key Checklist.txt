Stay Safe Online – Key Checklist

Using strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
Enabling the 2-factor authentication wherever possible.
Never sharing the UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
Always verifying payment requests, PIN is not required to receive money.
Avoiding clicking on unknown links from messages, emails, or social media.
Not using any public Wi-Fi for banking or payments, as they usually hides malware.
Downloading apps only from official app stores (avoiding third-party APKs).
Avoiding scanning random QR codes, especially from unknown sources.
Checking app permissions regularly and removing apps you don’t use.
Reporting a fraud immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal
   (cybercrime.gov.in) or calling Helpline 1930 in case of any scam.
