UPI / Online payment fraud

  This type of fraud is very common in nowadays since it involves the transaction of money and cashless payments to others. Payment fraud is very diverse in action , people get scammed and robbed because they have common connections on social media and the don't have stronger security to their payments and mostly due to lack of awareness.

Most common target in UPI frauds are people from very humble background and elders and some times kids who usually don't have proper knowledge of the digital payments and precautions .

A very common way to get scammed is by through unknown SMS, mails and websites from unidentified resources , fake OTPs , fake PINs and some times scammer pretend to be fake person from their registered banks.

A real case scenario is of my brother , he recently got scammed on Instagram where scammer pretends to be his friend by make a duplicate & fake Insta account of his friend and send him message requesting money , 1st he asked for less amount then kept increasing his demand until he (my brother) knew that something is wrong, but its too late.

The consequences of this type of frauds are greater than it seems , people loose their money instantly and its hard to recover and further more it causes stress and trust issues .
